## Overview

VerifiSaaS is an enterprise-grade email verification and deliverability intelligence platform designed to help businesses reduce bounce rates, protect sender reputation, and maximise campaign performance.

Modern email infrastructure requires clean, reliable data. VerifiSaaS ensures that every address entering your system is validated, analysed, and scored before being used in marketing campaigns, onboarding flows, CRM systems, or transactional messaging.

---

## Core Capabilities

### Real-Time Email Verification
Instantly validate whether an email address is active and capable of receiving messages.

### Bulk Email Validation
Upload large email lists for comprehensive cleaning and structured verification output.

### Deliverability Confidence Score
Each email receives a dynamic 0–100 confidence score indicating likelihood of inbox placement.

### Risk & Threat Detection
Identify disposable addresses, high-risk domains, and known spam trap patterns before they impact performance.

### Predictive Accept-All Analysis
Advanced analysis for domains that accept all emails to help optimise sending strategy.

### Privacy & Compliance Signals
Flag domains potentially subject to regional data protection regulations such as GDPR and CCPA.
